STAAR Test Maker’s Item Bank

To create assessments with STAAR Test Maker, teachers and administrators select test questions from our Texas-specific Item Bank. Our Item Bank consists of 30,000 of the highest-quality, up-to-date Reading, Mathematics, Writing, Science, and EOC items for use in Texas schools in grades 3 through 12.


All of our test items have been specifically written to the STAAR Readiness and Supporting Standards and the latest TEKS Expectations. All of our test items are written, edited, and designed by our team of experienced assessment writers and editors. Every question is written specifically for Texas, and specifically for STAAR Test Maker.
Our item bank is continually refined and updated, based upon user input and Texas Education Agency requirements. A Reliability and Validity Study is frequently performed to maintain the quality of our test items. Download our Reliability and Validity Study

Item Bank Licensing

Our high-quality, Texas-specific item bank is available for licensing in other software to meet your school district’s assessment needs.

Licensing our STAAR Test Maker item bank will give you as many as 30,000 of the best test questions that are aligned to the STAAR Readiness and Supporting Standards and the latest TEKS Expectations.

Our item bank can be supplied to you in MS Word, XML, or QTI formats. Optional use of STAAR Test Maker software is also available for District Assessment staff only.
