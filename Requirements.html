System Requirements

  • Desktop or laptop computer with at least a 13" monitor (486, 586, or Pentium processor)
  • Operating system must be Windows 98 or higher
  • 32 MB free RAM required
  • 300 MB of free hard drive space
  • CD drive
  • Printer
  • Apperson Advantage 1200 Scanner (if you choose to use Machine Scoring)

Software Features

  • Create and print realistic, data-driven STAAR tests in fewer than 3 minutes.
  • Data-driven, STAAR-specific Item Bank features an average of 2,000 questions per grade — more than 40 full-length STAAR Tests are just a few clicks away.
  • All questions adhere to the STAAR Readiness and Supporting Standards and new Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills (TEKS).
  • Includes valid Reading Passage types leveled by Lexile, Dale-Chall, and Spache, and more.
  • Produce easy-to-read, color coded reports FAST:
    • by Student
    • by Class
    • by Benchmark
    • by Grade
    • by Teacher
    • by NCLB Subgroup(s)
  • Select questions by STAAR Readiness and Supporting Standards.
  • Choose, order, scramble, and even select your own test items.
  • Includes machine scoring and scanner support.

Scanner support
