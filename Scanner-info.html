The Apperson Advantage 1200

If you choose to use machine scoring for your assessments, STAAR Test Maker supports the Apperson Advantage 1200 Scanner.

Scanner features:­­­­­

  • Score exams with up to 200 questions at 100 per pass
  • Low-cost answer sheets available in many formats
  • Alpha-numeric messages display for easy operation
  • Tallies the number of student answer sheets scored
  • Stand-alone or PC-compatible test scoring machine for advanced data collection and analysis

The Advantage 1200 test scanner is versatile and fast, scanning tests of up to 200 questions at 100 per pass. It can also plug in and scan on any classroom table, providing test results when you need them.

The Advantage 1200 is an affordable, reliable option for your scanning needs. To learn more about the Apperson Advantage 1200 Scanner, visit their website.
